IF YOU DO LIKE TEXT

I was born in 1989 and farted around childhood and the internet for some 18 years before attending Stanford University. While pursing my B.S. in Symbolic Systems, which interdisciplinary study of consciousness involved philosophy, psychology, linguistics, computer science and (particularly for me) no small amount of interface design, I realized that I was far less interested in "advancing my field" or "starting a company", than I was in engaging with people about the broader human issues my studies brought up. I also realized that datavisualizations were a very big part of the very exciting future of journalism and I wanted to start making them as well as I possibly could. So I tacked on an M.A. in Journalism, and graduated in June 2012.

I've also been up to many skill-and-character-and-career-building-very-professional-sounding things besides, and if you're really interested in everything I can do (illustrator! video! poetry! portuguese! janis joplin impressions!), please, look at my resume. Over there.

Also, I built this website in an incredibly short amount of time, if you're impressed with that kind of thing. Mainly, I am impressed with Bootstrap.
