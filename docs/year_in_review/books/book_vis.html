Halfway through the year I decided to start taking notes on the things I read, to stopper my mind's sieve. It was a good idea, but it didn't give me this kind of birds-eye-view of my literary influences. Contrary to popular belief, I read a lot more than just DFW. My 23-year-old self found nothing worth reading from the '40s and '50s. I probably did read Scott Hutchins' book faster than normal because he was coming over for dinner. Generaly, I would like to read more women, but in the meantime Anne Carson will do.
